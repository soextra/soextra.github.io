Zero Point Extraction

Zero Point Extraction is a locally owned and operated CBD (hemp) extraction company that focuses on wholesale production of CBD isolate, distillate, crude, and full spectrum oils. We source our biomass locally from a co-op of farms, and work with local and regional laboratories to supply wholesale markets worldwide. We are not a marijuana business; our products contain 0.0% THC. Zero Point Extraction is located in Central Oregon.

Core Values

Zero Point Extraction is built on integrity, sustainability, respect and vision. We strive to produce the highest quality CBD products in the wholesale market and to always remain a steadfast supplier for our partners and affiliates. Our mission is to scale our business with a focus on sustainability and incrementally reduce our carbon footprint until we achieve Zero Point emissions.
