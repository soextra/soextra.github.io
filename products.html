CBD Crude Oil

Crude oil contains a variety of essential vitamins, minerals, fatty acids, protein, chlorophyll, terpenes, flavonoids, and fiber.

Pure CBD Isolate

CBD refined to strip out any additional cannabinoids, terpenes, and plant components found in the hemp plant. The final product is a fine white powder that contains around 99% cannabidoil.

CBD Full Spectrum Distillate

A technique for creating cannabis extracts that are pure on a molecular level. It is possible to isolate specific cannabinoids. It is also possible to create odorless and tasteless CBD that is 99% pure concentrate. All products will be destined for distribution to downstream wholesale markets.
